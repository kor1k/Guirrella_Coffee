Reservation

A premium Restaurant HTML theme

We accept reservations for parties of up to 6 people. For parties of 7 or more, please call us at (00) 123 456 789 or

e-mail us at email@domain.com Parties of 7 or more are required to participate in our tasting menu and a credit card is

required to reserve the table.

Please Note

 

All reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the reserved time. After 15 minutes, we can no longer guarantee your

reservation, but will do our best to accommodate your party. The full menu is served in the dining room and the bar

until 10:30pm Sunday through Wednesday and until 11:30pm Thursday through Saturday.
